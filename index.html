Welcome to the Club of Indian Diaspora

The Club of Indian Diaspora (CID) celebrates the rich and vibrant culture of India. Our club brings together students who are interested in Indian traditions, festivals, music, food and cultural experiences.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a welcoming community where students can learn about Indian culture, share traditions, and celebrate diversity within our school.
